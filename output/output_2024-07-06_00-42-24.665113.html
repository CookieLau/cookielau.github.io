Bloomberg Academy in Singapore is an outstanding 4 day personal and professional development programme for high potential University students who are graduating from all studies between September 2025 and September 2026 and who are particularly interested in a career in Bloomberg and the financial markets. During the programme, you’ll also have a chance to meet and hear from our recent Analytics and Sales programme graduates to learn about what we do here and how to apply for our graduate and internship opportunities.

You will be exposed to a wide array of topics, people and activities. We will cover everything from financial instruments to Bloomberg Terminal functionality, public speaking skills, and more! You will learn from some of the best in the industry and know how to apply this knowledge in a practical, fast-paced business environment.

This is an amazing opportunity to learn all about our company, our unique culture and the different opportunities in store for you!

Read more about the Department
How Analytics fuels Bloomberg’s core mission
A Day in the Life of Analytics

Dates: July 22-25, 2024
Venue: Bloomberg Singapore Office

You will need to be available to join us in-person for the full duration to be considered.

What’s in it for you?

  • Introduction to Bloomberg and Analytics & Sales Department
  • Network, job shadow, and learn about a Day in the Life of our business representatives from Analytics & Sales Department
  • Introduction to Financial Markets and interactive training on the Bloomberg Terminal
  • Introduction to Bloomberg Philanthropies and volunteering work
  • Practical workshops on interview skills, including mock interviews with Bloomberg interviewers
  • Develop public speaking skills
  • Enhance your presentation skills
  • Access to Bloomberg Market Concepts (BMC), a 10-hour self-paced course that provides a visual introduction to the financial markets: Economics, Currencies, Fixed Income and Equities — woven together from Bloomberg data, news, analytics and television
  • Get an overview of our summer internship and graduate programmes and the respective application process

All attendees are guaranteed a future interview for Analytics & Sales 2025 Summer Internship Position if they successfully complete the Bloomberg Academy.

You’ll need to have:

  • Graduating from all studies between September 2025 and September 2026
  • Business proficiency and fluency in English
  • Demonstrable interest in financial markets and the aspiration for a future career in Bloomberg ‘Analytics & Sales’ department or to
  • work in the financial/fintech services industry
  • Ambition to pursue a client-facing career, with a strong interest in relationship management and sales
  • Ability to communicate effectively and articulate thought process clearly
  • Resilience to learn and adapt quickly in a dynamic fast-paced environment
  • All degree disciplines are welcome to apply. Our aim is to have a diverse intake of candidates.

We’d love to see:

  • Business proficiency and fluency in Japanese, to communicate with respective clients in the future

Application Process:

  • CV/Cover Letter Screening
  • Pre-Recorded Video Interview
  • Language Assessment for Japanese speaker

Bloomberg Academy (Data) in Singapore is an outstanding 4 day personal and professional development programme for high potential STEM related degrees University students who will be graduating between September 2024 and August 2026 and who are particularly interested in a career in Bloomberg Data department. During the programme, you’ll also have a chance to meet and hear from our Data employees to learn about what we do here and how to apply for our graduate opportunities.

You will be exposed to a wide array of topics, people and activities. We will cover everything from Bloomberg Terminal functionality, enhance your programming and data analytics skills, and more! You will learn from some of the best in the industry and know how to apply this knowledge in a practical, fast-paced business environment.

This is an amazing opportunity to learn all about our company, our unique culture and a potential career in Data for you!

Dates: July 30 - August 2, 2024
Venue: Bloomberg Singapore Office

You will need to be available to join us in-person and have the right to work for the full duration to be considered. Please note travel and accommodation will not be provided.

What’s in it for you?

  • Introduction to Bloomberg and Data Department
  • Introduction on applying data concepts to financial markets and interactive training on the Bloomberg Terminal
  • Technical project to enhance your programming, data management and analytical skills
  • Practical workshops on technical interview skills, including mock interviews with Bloomberg interviewers
  • Network, job shadow, and learn about a Day in the Life from members of our Data Department
  • Introduction to Bloomberg Philanthropies and volunteering work
  • Access to Bloomberg Market Concepts (BMC), a 10-hour self-paced course that provides a visual introduction to the financial markets: Economics, Currencies, Fixed Income and Equities — woven together from Bloomberg data, news, analytics and television
  • Get an overview of our graduate programmes and the respective application process
  • Bloomberg Academy (Data) is a great way to learn more about our company and explore a potential career in Data.

You’ll need to be:

  • Graduating between September 2024 and August 2026
  • Have a minimum of a bachelor's degree in Statistics, Computer Science, or other STEM related field
  • Experience using technical skills such as programming, data modeling and statistical methods to solve problem
  • Ability to prioritize and adapt to tasks accordingly
  • Collaboration skills to work across departments and with global teams
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Right to work in Singapore for the duration of the Bloomberg Academy (Data) - Singapore

We’d love to see:

  • Previous work, internship and/or academic experience in data analysis, financial market research and/or information technology
  • A passion and curiosity about financial and economic data with the aspiration to work in the financial/fintech services industry

Application Process:

  • CV Screening
  • Interview with a member of the recruitment team
