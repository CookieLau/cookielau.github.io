Starting your first job can be both an exciting and challenging experience.

From navigating a new work environment to meeting the expectations of your employer and colleagues, there are many factors that can contribute to feelings of anxiety and pressure. In fast-paced and high-pressure work environments, mental clarity is more important than ever. The ability to focus, think critically, and make sound decisions can make all the difference in achieving personal and professional success.

So how can you achieve mental clarity, get good sleep and maintain your productivity when dealing with emotional stressors that can surface at any time? In this session, you will acquire simple strategies that you can apply to help you be at your best at work.

Through this workshop, you will:

  • Learn practical strategies for enhancing mental clarity 
  • Develop skills and mindset to thrive in your career
  • Apply simple practices to pause and reflect in difficult situations
  • Learn how to manage your emotions and cultivate greater mental clarity to thrive in your personal and professional life

This session will be an interactive workshop. Come prepared to learn new ideas and practise with your peers!

Light refreshments will be provided.

Speaker


Brenda Tan
Wellness Coach, Speaker and Author

Brenda Tan is a Wellness Coach, Speaker, and Author of the best-selling book "Regain Your Power," with extensive experience in the corporate world and high-level client consultation. She is passionate about promoting mental clarity and emotional well-being, having been featured on media outlets such as 938FM, Straits Times, TODAY, and Capital TV Malaysia. 

Brenda has helped individuals and organisations cultivate greater mental clarity, discover their potential, and expand possibilities in all areas of life through her training and one-to-one consultations. Brenda also organises and facilitates Pause retreats and forest bathing (shinrin-yoku) sessions to help people calm their mind and come back to their senses. 

Her clients span across countries such as Australia, America, Canada, England, Malaysia, and Singapore. In her free time, Brenda volunteers for Singapore Women's Prison and mentors students at the Institute of Technical Education.

Manage Job Offers and Salary Negotiation 2023-10-16 2023-10-16 N/A üîó Others

Congratulations! You have been offered an internship or a job. Now what? 

After receiving a job offer, you will have to evaluate and manage the offer while considering various factors and possible scenarios. You will need to know your motivators, as well as the things to look out for in an employment contract and remuneration package. The next step is to accept or reject the offer professionally and ethically and apply appropriate negotiation techniques to achieve the best package.

In this interactive workshop, expert trainer Clement Ong will guide you in navigating the complexities of managing job offers and salary negotiation through case studies, small group discussions and facilitated reflection. This session will include live discussions in the main room and breakout rooms, where you will work on a case study, respond with your solutions and will be given live expert feedback.

In this workshop, you will learn to:

  • Identify your motivators and effectively evaluate employment contracts and remuneration packages
  • Professionally and ethically handle multiple job offers, mastering the essential Dos and Don’ts 
  • Learn the Dos and Don’ts in salary negotiation and develop effective responses to salary-related inquiries  
  • Understand different types of negotiation tips and techniques
  • Learn the art of gracefully accepting or declining an offer in a professional manner
  • Cultivate the ability to handle rejections with grace and professionalism 

Who should attend?

  • Students who are currently applying and receiving offers for internships or full-time roles
  • Spaces are limited for this session. Non-Business School students and Undergraduate students are given priority. For any Business School students interested to register, please register for the waiting list. For any Postgraduate student interested to attend, please register for the waiting list.

To foster an engaging learning environment, you will be required to activate your camera and interact with other participants. We kindly ask you to register only if you can participate fully in the session.

Speaker


Clement Ong
Career Coach & Trainer 

Clement Ong, the Founder and Director of Lighthouse Pte Ltd, has over 18 years of experience in providing quality and effective career management, career transition/outplacement and executive coaching programs to major MNCs and large listed organisations from a wide range of industries. He works with clients ranging from senior management to support-level employees locally and in the region.

He is a Certified Career Practitioner (CCP) with the Workforce Singapore and his designation of Career Management Fellow (CMF) is awarded by the Institute of Career Certification International (ICCI), USA. Clement facilitates career skills workshops with the major universities in Singapore.

The rapid advancement of technology has brought about disruptive changes, creating both challenges and remarkable opportunities.  In particular, digitalisation has created a new way of living and transformed the operations of businesses and brands.

The Digital Economy has been identified as one of the three high-growth economies in Singapore as part of the Skills Demand for the Future Economy Report. So what exactly does the “Digital Economy” entail, and how can students seize opportunities to thrive in this dynamic realm?

Join us for an enlightening panel session featuring esteemed guest speakers from Mastercard, CAD-IT Consultants – a leading consultancy for Industry 4.0 implementation, and a business leader who has helped drive Singapore's Solar, Electric Vehicle and SuperApp revolution. These experts will share their valuable insights and perspectives on how to prepare for and embrace the abundant opportunities in the Digital Economy. Discover how to navigate the challenges and position yourself for success. Additionally, take advantage of the networking opportunities at the end of the session to connect with the panelists and gain expert insights and advice.
 
In this session, you will:

  • Understand the diverse opportunities in the Digital Economy and how to prepare for roles in data analytics, e-commerce, product development, 3D Design, machine learning and many more  
  • Gain an overview of the typical business challenges encountered in these roles and the essential skillsets needed to meet them 
  • Identify the core technical and soft skills needed to succeed in competitive Tech and Fintech companies, as well as leading consultancies
  • Acquire invaluable advice from experienced professionals on the type of internships and experiences you need to gain opportunities in these competitive sectors
  • Network with the panellists and receive expert advice 

Who should attend?

  • All students who are keen to find out more about the Digital Economy.
