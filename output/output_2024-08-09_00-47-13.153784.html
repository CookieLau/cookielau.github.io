This workshop will familiarise you with key strategies for approaching case interviews, case structuring foundations, case math, and provide you with a chance to practice a full case in a group and receive valuable feedback from the facilitator.

The session will be concluded with a brief tour of CaseCoach, during which we will discuss how to efficiently and effectively utilise the platform to prepare for case interviews. All NUS students can register for complimentary access to the CaseCoach platform.

  • An introduction to case interviews, including where it is used, why it is used, format, competencies assessed
  • A full live case practice as a group, start to finish
  • A deep dive into case structuring, including what is structuring, what a good structure looks like: the AIM test, key academic frameworks
    interactive case structuring practice with 4-5+ case questions and coach feedback
  • A deep dive into case math with interactive practice, including 2-3+ math questions and coach feedback
  • Insights on how to prepare for success, including what a well-prepared candidate looks like and how to use the CaseCoach platform to prepare

  • Ahead of the session, students should log onto CaseCoach and familiarize themselves with the platform. We recommend watching the 5-minute Management Consulting Recruitment Process video and downloading the Case Interview Scorecard.
