NUS Business School

 All undergraduate students

MPA GIA is a talent development programme targeting local, high-achieving undergraduate students in their penultimate year from both maritime and non-maritime disciplines in universities. It is a 12-week programme with maritime companies from May to October and includes a stint at any of their overseas offices for up to 6 weeks. MPA will fully cover the GIA allowance, the costs of a return economy-class airfare, overseas accommodation, travel and travel-related expenses (e.g. insurance).

Find out more about this at the virtual sessions on 4 Dec or 11 Dec!
