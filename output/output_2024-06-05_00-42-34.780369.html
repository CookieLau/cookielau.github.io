You are invited to participate in our annual tech festival – BrainHack!

What is BrainHack?
BrainHack comprises six competitions - AI, cybersecurity, app development, space data exploitation, virtual/augmented reality and fake news detection. Since 2019, students from more than 70 schools participated in our event. BrainHack 2024’s theme is “Imagine What’s Next”. It aims to let participants discover the transformative potential of technology and innovation, and our collective power to shape the future.

What can you expect?
Event is free to join with no pre-requisite required. You can expect to receive training, hone industry-relevant skills, and win cash prizes from a total prize pool of S$120,000, distributed across the competitions. You can also connect with fellow techies, listen live to an astronaut sharing his works on International Space Station, play and get hands-on experience on the latest defence tech applications!

BrainHack is one of the springboards into the Defence Tech Community. DSTA values competency in a wide array of technical fields and strongly encourages students interested in a STEM or Defence Tech career to participate.

Where to register or find out more?
You can:

  • Register and find out more at https://go.gov.sg/dsta-brainhack-signup,
  • Stay up to date on the event at https://go.gov.sg/dsta-brainhack-telegram
  • Reach us at brainhack@dsta.gov.sg

Gather your friends and share the invites! We look forward to seeing you at the event!

During the programme, you’ll also have a chance to meet and hear from our Data employees to learn about what we do here and how to apply for our graduate opportunities.

You will be exposed to a wide array of topics, people and activities. We will cover everything from Bloomberg Terminal functionality, enhance your programming and data analytics skills, and more! You will learn from some of the best in the industry and know how to apply this knowledge in a practical, fast-paced business environment.

This is an amazing opportunity to learn all about our company, our unique culture and a potential career in Data for you!

Dates: July 30 - August 2, 2024
Venue: Bloomberg Singapore Office

You will need to be available to join us in-person and have the right to work for the full duration to be considered. Please note travel and accommodation will not be provided.

What’s in it for you?

  • Introduction to Bloomberg and Data Department
  • Introduction on applying data concepts to financial markets and interactive training on the Bloomberg Terminal
  • Technical project to enhance your programming, data management and analytical skills
  • Practical workshops on technical interview skills, including mock interviews with Bloomberg interviewers
  • Network, job shadow, and learn about a Day in the Life from members of our Data Department
  • Introduction to Bloomberg Philanthropies and volunteering work
  • Access to Bloomberg Market Concepts (BMC), a 10-hour self-paced course that provides a visual introduction to the financial markets: Economics, Currencies, Fixed Income and Equities — woven together from Bloomberg data, news, analytics and television
  • Get an overview of our graduate programmes and the respective application process
  • Bloomberg Academy (Data) is a great way to learn more about our company and explore a potential career in Data.

You’ll need to be:

  • Graduating between September 2024 and August 2026
  • Have a minimum of a bachelor's degree in Statistics, Computer Science, or other STEM related field
  • Experience using technical skills such as programming, data modeling and statistical methods to solve problem
  • Ability to prioritize and adapt to tasks accordingly
  • Collaboration skills to work across departments and with global teams
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Right to work in Singapore for the duration of the Bloomberg Academy (Data) - Singapore

We’d love to see:

  • Previous work, internship and/or academic experience in data analysis, financial market research and/or information technology
  • A passion and curiosity about financial and economic data with the aspiration to work in the financial/fintech services industry

Application Process:

  • CV Screening
  • Interview with a member of the recruitment team
