Do you aspire for a career in consulting? Then join us in this workshop to master case interviews, a common assessment tool adopted by consultancy firms. The workshop will be led by case interview expert Daniel Sia, a former Bain Consultant in Southeast Asia and Australia.

This workshop will familiarise you with key strategies for approaching case interviews, case structuring foundations, case math, and provide you with a chance to practice a full case in a group and receive valuable feedback from the facilitator.

The session will be concluded with a brief tour of CaseCoach, during which we will discuss how to efficiently and effectively utilise the platform to prepare for case interviews. All NUS students can register for complimentary access to the CaseCoach platform.

Through this workshop, you will have:

  • An introduction to case interviews, including where it is used, why it is used, format, competencies assessed
  • A full live case practice as a group, start to finish
  • A deep dive into case structuring, including what is structuring, what a good structure looks like: the AIM test, key academic frameworks
    interactive case structuring practice with 4-5+ case questions and coach feedback
  • A deep dive into case math with interactive practice, including 2-3+ math questions and coach feedback
  • Insights on how to prepare for success, including what a well-prepared candidate looks like and how to use the CaseCoach platform to prepare

Requirements:

  • Ahead of the session, students should log onto CaseCoach and familiarize themselves with the platform. We recommend watching the 5-minute Management Consulting Recruitment Process video and downloading the Case Interview Scorecard.

Congratulations! You have been offered an internship or a job. Now what?

After receiving a job offer, you will have to evaluate and manage the offer while considering various factors and possible scenarios. You will need to know your motivators, as well as the things to look out for in an employment contract and remuneration package. The next step is to accept or reject the offer professionally and ethically and apply appropriate negotiation techniques to achieve the best package.

In this interactive workshop, expert trainer Clement Ong will guide you in navigating the complexities of managing job offers and salary negotiation through case studies, small group discussions and facilitated reflection. This session will include live discussions in the main room and breakout rooms, where you will work on a case study, respond with your solutions and will be given live expert feedback.

In this workshop, you will learn to:

  • Identify your motivators and effectively evaluate employment contracts and remuneration packages
  • Professionally and ethically handle multiple job offers, mastering the essential Dos and Don’ts
  • Learn the Dos and Don’ts in salary negotiation and develop effective responses to salary-related inquiries
  • Understand different types of negotiation tips and techniques
  • Learn the art of gracefully accepting or declining an offer in a professional manner
  • Cultivate the ability to handle rejections with grace and professionalism
