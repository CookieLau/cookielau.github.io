Greetings from Business China Youth Forum Organising Committee!

The Business China Youth Chapter (BCYC) warmly invites NUS students & staff to participate in the Business China Youth Forum 2023. The title of our upcoming forum is “New Horizons: Trailblazers Shaping the Pathways of Tomorrow”, and we are expecting a diverse group of youths from Singapore, China and the region to discuss up-and-rising opportunities and challenges in our region.

Business China Youth Forum (BCYF) has been organised in tandem with the FutureChina Global Forum (FCGF) since 2019 by the BCYC members. It aims to bring together youths in the ASEAN-China region to discuss in-depth critical issues and trends which are of interest to youths, and to provide the latest updates on exciting developments in China. This year, the forum will focus on three key trends in the ASEAN-China region:

Unlocking AI: Exploring Frontiers of Innovations

Empowering Youth Ambassadors: Bridging Divides in a Diverse World

Wo-men: Deep Dive into the Dynamic World of Women in Tech

This forum will be conducted in both English and Mandarin. Two-way simultaneous interpretation will be provided. Pre-registration for the simultaneous interpretation headset is required for physical attendance. Please indicate accordingly in the registration form.

It is possible to attend the forum in-person or virtually. You may scan the QR codes in the EDM to register accordingly.

Successful registrants will receive a confirmation email after the closing dates on 24 October (for physical attendance) and 26 October (for virtual attendance).

Should you have any other queries, please email to youthforum@businesschina.org.sg.
